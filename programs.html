Programs

Designed to meet the needs of the home owner

Oberstadt Landscapes lawn maintenance services can be provided on a weekly, monthly, or bimonthly basis and can include the following:

  • Removal of weed growth from the perennial, annual and shrub beds.
  • Debris & weed removal from driveway & walkways.
  • Fertilization of perennials & annuals.
  • Inspection of plant material to identify & diagnose pests & disease.
  • Treatment (if necessary) of plant material to minimize damage caused by pests and disease.
  • Prune shrubs/trees to remove unwanted growth, dead wood, problem limbs, shaping.
  • Edge planting beds, walks, drives, patio areas.
  • Deadhead perennials/annuals as needed.
  • Water plants and/or turf areas.
  • Deer and rabbit protection.
  • Clearing gutters.
  • Bird bath, fountain, and pond care.

To get started or schedule a consultation of your property email jerry@oberstadt.com.
