Landscape Design

A Unique Approach Design

Jerry's design process begins as soon as he hear about your project. He will schedule a consultation at your property to discuss your landscape vision. Each project is carefully planned out with your needs, personality and style taken into account. Jerry takes a "whole picture" approach, looking at all aspects of your home environment including home location and decor.

Jerry has an interest in current design trends including architecture, fashion and exterior design. He incorporate these current trends into an innovative landscape plan.

Once the design is complete Jerry will schedule a follow up meeting to present your landscape plan and make any necessary changes as needed.

How to Get Started:

For information or to set up a consultation appointment, please email jerry@oberstadt.com.
